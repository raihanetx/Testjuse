200% Increase in Conversions for Fashion Retailer

How we helped a struggling online fashion store transform their digital presence and boost sales.

Client Fashion Retailer
Industry E-commerce
Services UX Design, SEO, PPC
Timeline 6 Months
200% Conversion Rate Increase
45% Organic Traffic Growth
3.2x ROI on Ad Spend

The Challenge

Our client, a mid-sized fashion retailer with both physical stores and an online presence, was facing significant challenges with their e-commerce performance:

  • Low conversion rate (0.8% compared to industry average of 2.5%)
  • High cart abandonment rate (over 85%)
  • Poor mobile experience (60% of their traffic came from mobile devices)
  • Declining organic traffic due to outdated SEO practices
  • Inefficient ad spend with low return on investment

Despite having quality products and competitive pricing, the retailer was struggling to translate website visitors into customers. They approached us to diagnose the issues and implement a comprehensive solution to improve their online performance.

Our Approach

We began with a thorough analysis of the client's website, marketing channels, and customer journey to identify pain points and opportunities for improvement.

1. UX Audit and Website Redesign

Our first priority was addressing the poor user experience that was causing high bounce and abandonment rates:

  • Conducted user testing to identify friction points in the shopping journey
  • Redesigned the product pages with a focus on simplicity and clear calls-to-action
  • Implemented a mobile-first approach to ensure optimal experience across all devices
  • Reduced page load times by 60% through image optimization and code improvements

Before

  • Cluttered product pages with excessive information
  • 5-step checkout process
  • 4.5 second average page load time
  • Poor mobile navigation

After

  • Clean, minimalist product pages focusing on key details
  • 3-step streamlined checkout process
  • 1.8 second average page load time
  • Intuitive mobile-first navigation

2. SEO Optimization

To address declining organic traffic, we implemented a comprehensive SEO strategy:

  • Conducted keyword research to identify high-intent search terms in the fashion niche
  • Optimized product category pages for relevant keywords
  • Created a content strategy focusing on fashion guides and trend articles
  • Implemented technical SEO improvements including schema markup for products
  • Built quality backlinks from fashion blogs and industry publications

3. Targeted Remarketing Campaigns

To recover abandoned carts and re-engage potential customers:

  • Implemented segmented email remarketing sequences based on cart value and products
  • Created dynamic remarketing ads showing products users had viewed
  • Developed a strategic discount ladder offering increasing incentives over time
  • Implemented exit-intent popups with targeted offers

The Results

Within six months of implementing our strategy, the client saw dramatic improvements across all key metrics:

Conversion Rate

The conversion rate increased from 0.8% to 2.4% (a 200% improvement), bringing the site in line with industry benchmarks. This was primarily driven by the improved user experience and streamlined checkout process.

Organic Traffic

Organic traffic grew by 45% compared to the previous period, with a 60% increase in traffic to optimized category pages. The new content strategy also resulted in 15,000 additional monthly visitors from informational searches.

Ad Performance

The ROI on advertising spend increased from 1.2x to 3.2x, largely due to more effective targeting and the remarketing campaigns. Cart abandonment recovery emails achieved a 15% conversion rate, significantly above industry averages.

Revenue Impact

The combined effect of these improvements resulted in a 180% increase in monthly online revenue, transforming the e-commerce channel from an underperforming segment to a major revenue driver for the business.

Key Takeaways

This case study demonstrates several important principles that can be applied to other e-commerce businesses:

  • User experience is paramount - Even small friction points in the shopping journey can significantly impact conversion rates
  • Mobile optimization is non-negotiable - With the majority of traffic coming from mobile devices, a mobile-first approach is essential
  • Recovery strategies work - Implementing systems to recover abandoned carts can substantially increase revenue with minimal additional cost
  • Integrated approach yields best results - The combination of UX improvements, SEO, and targeted advertising created a multiplier effect on performance
