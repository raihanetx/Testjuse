E-commerce

200% Increase in Conversions for Fashion Retailer

200% Conversion Rate Increase
45% Organic Traffic Growth
3.2x ROI on Ad Spend

The Challenge

Our client, a mid-sized fashion retailer, was struggling with low conversion rates and high cart abandonment despite having quality products and competitive pricing.

Our Approach

We implemented a comprehensive strategy that included:

  • Complete UX audit and website redesign focusing on simplicity
  • Streamlined checkout process reducing steps by 40%
  • Targeted remarketing campaigns to recover abandoned carts
  • SEO optimization for key product categories

The Results

Within 6 months, the client saw a 200% increase in conversion rate, 45% growth in organic traffic, and a 3.2x return on ad spend.
